# Sekhar Albums Management

Developing an end-to-end management platform for photography studios to track album projects, financial transactions, and studio performance.

- **Project Lifecycle Tracking**: Manage album projects through various stages: Pending, Running, Completed, and Delivered.
- **Dynamic Dashboard Analytics**: Real-time visualization of revenue trends, project distributions, and top-performing studios using interactive charts.
- **Financial Management**: Automated tracking of project totals, advances collected, and pending balances with multi-currency (INR) support.
- **Studio & Client Management**: Centralized database for studio partners, including location tracking and historical project performance.
- **Invoice & Report Generation**: One-click PDF invoice generation for clients and CSV export capabilities for business auditing.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Granular permission system ensuring secure access for administrators, designers, and studio partners.
